What is LoTV 4

Lily of The Valley 4 with its subtitle Field Of Ease become the fourth installment of the LoTV novels series, following the writers story on a camping trip while school holiday. Later, he meets with his new friends name Mayu, a girl who is easy to be friends with.


Later, Field Of Ease becomes it's own single novels book series resulting the Field Of Ease 2. It's a completely another whole stories that have a very small gap between becoming a canon in the series or not. To know more about Field Of Ease 2 check it out on Images pages or Other Work pages.


About The Images

The images show a trail that is unknown where it go, it's a stock picture used by the writter to avoid from getting a copyright when the book was planned to be publish on AKDP (Which Is Not Anymore). This is design reflect the setting of the story where it set in a natural environment that the writter explores, as he goes through a deep emotion of life and nature.
